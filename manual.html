Renting a Car

  1. Have an account: Login if it already exists or Register if not.
  2. Search for the car you want to rent using the search bar.
  3. Press the Rent button in the table beside the car you chose.

Returning a Car

  1. Open the Current Reservations page.
  2. Select the reservation you want to return (when the time limit for your rent is up).
  3. Press the Return button.

Pickup Process

  1. Go to the office location shown after the reservation of the car on the pickup day.
  2. Show them your registration info which is available on the site.

Payment Process

  1. After selecting a car and pressing the Rent button, you will be redirected to a payment page.
  2. Submit the required information: pickup time and return time.
  3. Payment is required before reserving the ride.
